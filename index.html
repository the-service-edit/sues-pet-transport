The Person Behind Every Visit

Hi, I'm Sue

Animals have been my world for as long as I can remember. I grew up surrounded by dogs, cats, rabbits and just about every creature you can imagine.

I started this business because I saw how stressful it was for pet owners to find someone they could genuinely trust. Whether it was a vet visit, a week away, or a rescue that needed a safe pair of hands, people deserved better.

Every service I offer is personal. I get to know your pet before we work together. I understand their temperament, their comfort needs, their little quirks. And I keep you updated the entire time.

The rescue and rehoming work is where my heart really is. Giving vulnerable animals a safe landing and matching them with the right forever family — there's nothing more rewarding.

— Sue
